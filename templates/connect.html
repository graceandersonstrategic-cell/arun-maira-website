A Note on Digital Presence

These channels are maintained as quiet extensions of the reading room. They are not monitored for comments or conversation, but exist simply as additional pathways for encountering the work. The intention is to meet readers where they are, without demanding their attention or encouraging noise.

For direct inquiries from institutions, editors, or event organizers, please use the contact form. For personal readers, the reading room remains the deepest source of Arun's writing and thinking.

Clarification on Arun's Approach

As Arun notes: "I work on my own, apply my own mind, and write my own way. Hence my reluctance to do too much, and to focus on the essence—the essentials." This selective presence reflects his focus on substance over visibility.
