How These Buckets Connect

From The System to Me/Us: How large-scale systems affect individual lives and relationships. For example, economic systems shape employment opportunities which in turn affect personal purpose.

From Me/Us to The System: How personal values and relationships can influence and transform larger systems. Listening and dialogue can change organizational cultures and policies.

All Roads Lead to India's Future: Both systemic understanding and personal values inform India's development path. A sustainable future for India requires both technical solutions and human connections.
