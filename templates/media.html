Speaking & Events

Event Information

Arun speaks occasionally at academic institutions, policy forums, and leadership conferences. His talks typically focus on systems thinking, governance, economic reimagining, and the future of work and care.

Typical Formats

  • Keynote addresses (45-60 minutes)
  • Panel discussions and moderated dialogues
  • Academic lectures and seminars
  • Executive workshops on systems thinking

Inquiry Process

  • Please use the contact form
  • Include event details, audience, and format
  • Allow several weeks for consideration
  • Arun accepts only a limited number of engagements

For Academic & Research Inquiries

Academic researchers and students are welcome to reference Arun's work in their research. For in-depth research inquiries, interviews, or requests for unpublished materials, please use the contact form and include details about your research project, institution, and intended use of the material.

Citation Style

Please cite published works according to standard academic citation formats. Book citations should include publisher and year information.

Permissions

For permission to reproduce extended excerpts or full articles, please contact the original publishers directly, as copyright is typically held by publishers.

Contact for Press Inquiries

For urgent press inquiries or interview requests, please use the contact form and indicate "PRESS INQUIRY" in the subject line. Include your outlet, deadline, and the focus of your proposed piece.

Note: Arun does not provide comments on breaking news or current events, but is available for considered conversations about the themes central to his work.
